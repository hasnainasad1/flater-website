Services

What We Do

Furniture and Upholstery

AZ Showtime offers upholstery cleaning. We can restore the color and beauty of your favorite couch, love-seat, mattress or chair. We will leave your home making it look, smell, and feel fresh and new again. Eco-friendly products available.

Carpet Cleaning

We will provide you with a free on-site estimate for commercial carpet, tile and grout and upholstery cleaning. We work around your schedule. When your office is closed, we clean. We will start working once the building is vacant and ensure that it is ready to go before the start of the next business day.

Area Rugs

Our trained technician will restore the patterns and colors of your area rug. Regular cleaning will restore the appearance and extend the life of your area rugs while also leaving your home cleaner and healthier for you and your family. Eco-friendly products available.

Tile and Grout

Your tile and grout floors get a regular mopping to keep them clean. Overtime, however, soil absorbs into the grout lines and tile floors begin to lose the shine.AZ Showtimes technicians use a powerful truck-mounted equipment and tools specially designed to get your tile and grout lines looking fresh and clean again. Eco-friendly products available.
